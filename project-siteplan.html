Site Plan: Exploring Nigerian Culture and Lifestyle

Subject

Exploring Nigerian Culture and Lifestyle

This website will explore Nigeria’s culture and daily life, including traditional foods, clothing, festivals, and popular places to visit. It will include sections on local dishes, cultural traditions, and tourist attractions, along with images and interactive features.

Reason

I chose this topic because I am from Nigeria and I want to share the beauty of my culture and lifestyle with others. It is a topic I understand well, and it will allow me to create meaningful and engaging content.

Site Name

Discover Nigerian Life

This name highlights the focus on cultural exploration, traditions, and lifestyle in Nigeria.

Optional domain availability: discovernigerianlife.org

Site Purpose

The site provides a hub for learning about Nigerian culture, daily life, and tourism. It will feature information about traditional foods, clothing, festivals, cultural customs, and popular tourist destinations.

Scenarios

  • What are Nigeria’s most popular traditional dishes?
  • Which festivals are unique to Nigeria and when are they celebrated?
  • Where can tourists visit to experience Nigerian culture?

Color Schema

  • Dark Green (#006400) – headings, navigation bar, footer
  • Orange (#ff8c00) – accents for buttons, cards, hero section
  • Light Gray (#f4f4f4) – background of the page

Typography

  • Georgia – headings (h1, h2)
  • Arial – body text (paragraphs)
  • Verdana – optional for small captions or nav links
